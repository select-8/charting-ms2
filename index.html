Presented here are the results of a survay of 800 professional developers from across the 28 countries of the European Union. For each of the countries surveyed, you will see what the most popular programming languages are and if there a correlation with average saleries, what the most preferred database systems in use are and how, while trends seem to changing, there remains a gender divide at the most senior levels. To debunk some myths we also asked about pastimes, and gaming in particular. Also, for a bit of fun, we decided to ask developers what their favourite Logical Operator is, this can be tracked in the number display above (can you spot any outliers!).
